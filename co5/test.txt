Ilahia College of Engineering and Technology (ICET) is a co-educational, 
self-financing engineering college in Mulavoor, Muvattupuzha, Kerala, established in 2002. 
It is approved by the AICTE and affiliated with the APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University (KTU). 
he college aims to provide academic excellence and holistic development through a curriculum focused on innovation, 
career advancement, and entrepreneurship.
